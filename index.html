Our Purpose

The BottlePoints: Bottle Collection and Reward System aims to collect recyclable bottles that can be turned to points, and the gathered points can be exchanged for tangible items.

This system also commited in promoting environmental awareness among the students, professors, and personnel through this project.

Through advocating about bottle collection activities in Colegio De Montalban, we can have a positive effect on the environment, and also encourage others to follow. Let's help each other now, Cdmians!

Our Features

  • Bottle Deposit Tracking

    Users can view their total bottles deposited, which is updated with each contribution. This feature offers real-time feedback on their collecting activities.

  • Points Allocation

    Displays earned points, allowing users to check their acquired points from bottle deposits.

  • Environmental Contributions

    Users can view their contributions to reduce carbon emissions based on the bottles they have collected. It also highlights the positive impact on energy consumption, the trees planted, and transportation.

  • The Leaderboard

    The top collectors are highlighted in this section. Users are ranked based on the number of bottles they return, fostering friendly competition among Cdmians.

  • Rewards History

    It provides a detailed record of all claimed rewards, allowing you to track and monitor your progress.

  • School Environmental Programs

    Updates users on upcoming events related to environmental awareness, such as seminars, recycling drives, or sustainability workshops. Stay tuned for more!
