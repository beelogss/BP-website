2. Eligibility

Only registered students of Colegio de Montalban are eligible to participate in the BottlePoints System.
Accounts are personal and should not be shared or transferred.

3 . Collecting and Earning Points

You can earn points by collecting recyclable bottles. Bottles must still have their barcode intact before being placed in the bin.
Points are awarded only for bottles that meet the system's specified types and attributes.

4. Redeeming Points

You may redeem your points for rewards, such as school supplies, that are presented in the BottlePoints System.
Rewards and their availability are subject to change based on stock and partnerships with supporting organizations.

5. Account Security

It is your responsibility to keep your account details secure.
