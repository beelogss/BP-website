Our Purpose

The BottlePoints: Barcode-based Recycling System aims to collect recyclable bottles that can be turned to points, and the gathered points can be exchanged for tangible items.

This system also commited in promoting recycling awareness among the students, professors, and personnel.

Through advocating about recycling in Colegio De Montalban, we can have a positive effect on the environment, and also encourage others to follow. Let's help each other now, Cdmians!
